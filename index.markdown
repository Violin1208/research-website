Deborah is a PhD candidate at UCL and her PhD is part of a collaborative project between the British Museum and UCL. Her research interests are in the areas of machine learning in Python, Natural Language Processing (NLP), knowledge representation, visualisation, network analysis and Collections as Data (CAD).

She also teaches part-time at UCL and leads the Learning and Teaching team for 'She Loves Data' in EMEA region. 

Deborah loves working with data and is passionate about empowering women with data and technology.

You can find the source code for Deborah's projects at [GitHub](https://github.com/Violin1208)
